Lanchester's Laws Theory

Linear Law (Melee)

In close combat, each soldier can only engage one enemy at a time. Casualties are directly proportional to army size.

casualties ∝ N

Square Law (Ranged)

In ranged combat, multiple soldiers can focus fire on the same target. Combat effectiveness scales quadratically.

effectiveness ∝ N²
